The History of
CHILLICOTHE IRON & STEEL, INC

Chillicothe Iron & Steel, Inc. has twenty-five of the most highly qualified employees. Nestled in the quiet rural town of Chillicothe, MO, we produce buildings for the Midwest. We have been a family-owned business since 1967 producing quality structural steel. Being a family-owned business, we have very little change in personnel. As shown left and below, our outside facility of 27,000 sq ft is capable of storing steel for several projects.

Our production area of 41,240 sq ft is designed for optimum efficiency. Material is sawed and punched as it enters the facility, moved to the welding station, and then finally painted and loaded.
