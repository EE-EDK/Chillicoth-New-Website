The History of Chillicothe Iron & Steel, Inc.

The original home of Chillicothe Iron & Steel, Inc.

Our current facility.

Chillicothe Iron & Steel, Inc. has twenty-five of the most highly qualified employees. Nestled in the quiet rural town of Chillicothe, MO, we produce buildings for the Midwest. We have been a family-owned business since 1967 producing quality structural steel. Being a family-owned business, we have very little change in personnel. As shown left and below, our outside facility of 27,000 sq ft is capable of storing steel for several projects.

Our production area of 41,240 sq ft is designed for optimum efficiency. Material is sawed and punched as it enters the facility, moved to the welding station, and then finally painted and loaded.

Equipment & Capabilities

Lifting & Handling

We have a variety of cranes and lifts to handle materials throughout the facility, including a Gallion crane and multiple overhead cranes ranging from 2 to 10 tons.

Cutting & Shearing

Our cutting and shearing capabilities include a 1999 HEMSAW vertical band saw, a Marvel 81 band saw, a 1 1/2" x 12' shearer, and a 500 ton 20' press break.

Welding & Fabrication

We use both automatic and manual welding equipment, including multiple Lincoln and wire-feed welders. We also have a Plasma Table for precision burning.
